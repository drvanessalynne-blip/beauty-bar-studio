# PROJECT_RULES.md — Beauty Bar Studio Constitution

This document is the project's constitution. Every future design, development, UX, SEO, accessibility, branding, animation, and architectural decision must follow these rules.

---

## Project Mission

Beauty Bar Studio should become one of the most beautiful luxury salon websites in Dallas.

The experience should feel like walking into a luxury spa rather than browsing a website.

Every decision should prioritize elegance, professionalism, warmth, trust, and exceptional user experience.

---

## Brand Personality

The brand is:

- Luxury
- Elegant
- Editorial
- Modern
- Sophisticated
- Minimal
- Warm
- Timeless
- Premium
- Calm

Avoid:

- Generic AI templates
- Clutter
- Loud colors
- Cheap effects
- Busy layouts
- Over-designed interfaces
- Trendy gimmicks

---

---

## Design Philosophy

Design inspiration should come from:

- Four Seasons
- Aesop
- Apple
- Soho House
- Drybar
- Luxury boutique hotels

The design should be calm, spacious, elegant, and intentional.

Whitespace is a design element.

---

## Layout Rules

- Use generous spacing.
- Balanced composition.
- Rounded corners.
- Soft shadows.
- Large imagery.
- Editorial layouts.
- Never crowd sections.

---

## Animation Rules

**Allowed:**
- Fade
- Scale
- Opacity
- Parallax
- Soft hover
- Glass blur

**Never use:**
- Bounce
- Flash
- Shake
- Spin
- Rapid movement

Use only subtle animations.

---

## Mobile First

Every feature must work perfectly on:

- Desktop
- Laptop
- Tablet
- Mobile

Buttons must be easy to tap.

Navigation must remain intuitive.

---

## Accessibility

- Target WCAG 2.2 AA.
- Semantic HTML.
- ARIA labels.
- Keyboard navigation.
- Proper focus states.
- Accessible color contrast.

---

## Performance

Target Lighthouse:

- Performance 95+
- Accessibility 100
- SEO 100
- Best Practices 100

- Lazy loading.
- Optimized assets.
- Minimal JavaScript.

---

## SEO

Optimize for:

- Beauty Salon Dallas
- Luxury Salon Dallas
- Day Spa Dallas
- Hair Salon Dallas
- Pilates Studio Dallas
- Nail Bar Dallas
- Microblading Dallas
- Waxing Dallas
- Bridal Beauty Dallas

Every page should include proper metadata and schema.

---

## AEO

Write naturally for AI-powered search.

Optimize for:

- Google AI
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity

Use conversational headings and FAQ sections.

---

## Booking

Primary booking platform:

**Square Appointments**

- Booking should never require more than two clicks.
- Book Now buttons should be visible throughout the site.

---

## User Experience

Visitors should always know:

- Who Beauty Bar Studio is
- What services are offered
- Why the business is different
- How to book
- How to make contact

Booking should always feel effortless.

---

## Code Philosophy

- Prefer reusable components.
- Avoid duplicate code.
- Maintain consistent naming.
- Favor readability over cleverness.
- Build for long-term maintainability.

---

## Future Features

Architect the project so it can easily support:

- Online Store
- Memberships
- Gift Cards
- Client Portal
- Blog
- Rewards Program
- Virtual Consultations
- AI Beauty Advisor
- Multiple Locations
- Franchise Expansion

---

## Final Rule

Whenever there are multiple possible solutions, choose the one that best improves:

- User Experience
- Maintainability
- Accessibility
- Performance
- SEO
- Brand Consistency
- Luxury Presentation
